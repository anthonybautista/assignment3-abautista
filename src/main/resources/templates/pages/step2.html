Step 2: Creating Your Wallet

Creating a new wallet with MetaMask is easy! Once your extension is installed open it up and click "Get Started".

Follow the prompts

MetaMask will take you through the process of setting up your wallet. Be sure to create a secure password and WRITE DOWN YOUR SEED PHRASE. Keep in mind that your seed phrase is THE KEY to your wallet. If you lose this phrase you will lose access to your wallet!
